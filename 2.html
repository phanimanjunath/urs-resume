Basic Tips for a Classy Resume:

  • ✅ Keep it one page (max 2 if necessary)
  • ✅ Use a clean and modern font like Calibri or Arial
  • ✅ Highlight key achievements, not just responsibilities
  • ✅ Keep formatting consistent: same bullet styles, fonts, alignments
  • ✅ Use action verbs like “Led”, “Created”, “Improved”
  • ✅ Avoid too many colors or graphics unless it’s for a creative field
